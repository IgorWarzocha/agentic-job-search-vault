# Interview Prep Directory

Prepare for specific interviews and common questions.

## Structure
- `Common-Questions/`: Answers to standard questions (e.g., "Tell me about yourself", "Your greatest weakness").
- `Company-Specific/`: Tailored preparation for upcoming interviews.
- `Presentation-Prep/`: Materials for presentation-based interview stages.

## Preparation Checklist
1. Review the Job Description.
2. Align achievements from your `CANDIDATE-PROFILE.md` to role requirements.
3. Research the interviewers.
4. Prepare 3-5 questions for the panel.
