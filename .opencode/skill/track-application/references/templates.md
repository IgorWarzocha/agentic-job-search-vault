## Follow-up Templates

### Gentle Follow-up (1 week after application)

```markdown
Subject: Following up - [Role] Application

Hi [Hiring Manager],

I hope this email finds you well.

I'm following up on my application for the [Role] position submitted on [date]. I'm genuinely excited about this opportunity and believe my [key skill/experience] would be valuable for [company need].

Would you be open to a quick chat about how I can contribute to your team?

Best regards,
[Candidate Name]
```

### Post-Interview Follow-up

```markdown
Subject: Great meeting - [Role] Interview

Hi [Interviewer],

Thank you again for the insightful conversation about the [Role] position.

I particularly enjoyed discussing [specific topic] and learning more about [company insight]. I'm even more convinced that my [key strength] would help you achieve [specific goal].

Looking forward to hearing about next steps.

Best regards,
[Candidate Name]
```
