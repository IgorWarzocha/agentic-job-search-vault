# Job Market Research Directory

This directory stores market intelligence and company-specific dossiers.

## Subdirectories
- `Active-Leads/`: Track open job postings and potential opportunities.
- `Company-Research/`: In-depth dossiers on companies of interest.
- `Sector-Analysis/`: High-level reports on industry trends and shifts.
- `Salary-Market-Data/`: Benchmarks for compensation and benefits.

## Best Practices
- Keep research objective and evidence-led.
- Use OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) techniques.
- Update regularly as market conditions change.
